Biography

  • My name is Robert Carter.
  • I am an Army veteran from the East Coast.
  • I have a Bachelor's Degree in Political Science, and I am halfway through a Master's in Public Policy.
  • While I was in the military, I was a Signal Corps Officer.
  • After I left the service I contracted with the Department of Defense and the Department of Homeland Security, both in a logistical capacity.
  • I moved to Washington State from D.C. about 18 months ago and have worked in managerial roles since.
  • I decided to move into coding because it has always been an interest of mine and, through a lot of factors, was denied the opportunity to pursue this career through both the military and government.
  • I hope to take what I learn here and use it to study cyber security, with the overall aim of becoming a "white hat".
